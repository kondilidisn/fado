How We Work

Planning that You Can See

We believe you should never have to imagine your wedding alone. Every conversation ends with a sketch — a real, hand-drawn image of what we're building together.

01

The First Sketch

In your very first session, we draw as we listen. Your words become lines on paper — ceremony layouts, table arrangements, arch designs, all taking shape in real time.

02

Palette & Theme

Together we define a color palette that will flow through every element — florals, linens, ribbons, candles. A single thread connecting every detail.

03

Detail Refinement

Each session refines the picture. We zoom in on specifics — the height of centerpieces, the arc of the aisle, the texture of the stationery — until everything feels right.

04

Flawless Execution

On your day, nothing is left to chance. Every vendor, every timing, every placement — executed exactly as sketched, with our eye on every detail.
