The Sketch Process

See It Before It Exists

At Fado, every consultation ends with something tangible. We sketch your wedding details live — ceremony arches, table layouts, floral arrangements — so you leave each session with a real image in your hands.

This isn't decoration. It's clarity. When you can see it, you can truly decide — and together we refine every line until it's exactly right.

  • Ceremony architecture drawn from your first words
  • Table arrangements sketched to scale, with dimensions
  • Floral compositions shown as loose botanical studies
  • Color notes annotated directly on the sketch
  • Revisions made live, session by session
