The Fado Process

We Sketch Your Dream Into Reality

During every consultation, we put pencil to paper — creating live sketches of your ceremony arch, table arrangements, floral details, and venue layouts as we talk through your vision.

This isn't just planning. It's a conversation with a picture, so you can truly see — and feel — each choice before it's made.

  • 1
    The Vision Session — We listen, and we draw. Your ideas become sketches in real time.
  • 2
    Palette & Theme — Together we choose your colors, textures, and the thread that ties everything together.
  • 3
    Detail by Detail — Every element — from napkin folds to centerpiece heights — is reviewed for harmony.
  • 4
    Your Perfect Day — We bring it all to life, exactly as imagined.
