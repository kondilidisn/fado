The Fado Method

Planning You Can See and Feel

◆

We believe a wedding planner should do more than manage logistics. She should be an artist — one who helps you see your day clearly before it arrives.

We sketch your wedding as we talk about it

Most wedding planning happens in the abstract — lists, spreadsheets, vendor emails. At Fado, it happens on paper, in front of you, in real time.

During every consultation, we draw. Your ceremony arch takes shape before your eyes. Your table arrangement finds its proportions. The scale and mood of your day become visible — not imagined, but seen.

This is the most powerful gift we can offer: clarity. When you can see it, you can truly decide. And when you truly decide, the day becomes exactly what you always wanted.

Step 01
The First Conversation

You speak, we listen — and we draw. Your vision becomes visible in the first session itself. By the end, you leave with something in your hands.

Step 02
Choosing Your Palette

Together we select your wedding's color soul — the palette that will flow through every ribbon, petal, and table linen, binding everything together.

Step 03
Detail by Detail

Each session refines the picture further. No detail is too small. The height of the centerpieces. The texture of the napkin. The arc of the aisle. All examined through the lens of your palette.

Step 04
The Perfect Execution

On the day, we are present — overseeing every detail with the precision and care that only comes from having planned it, sketched it, and lived it with you.
